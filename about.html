About Biasa Adja

The Biasa Adja Team is a Group of Minecraft Texture Pack Developer under the IndoRise Studios. In The End of 2021, Reynard (a.k.a The Co-Owner of IndoRise) planning making a Minecraft Texture Pack to publish and making a different Team, at First the CEO refuse it, but after some talking The CEO finnaly aggred. At First the Biasa Adja Team led by Reynard was originally planned to make Texture for our self. But in V3 Reynard decided to publish the texture pack publicly. As more Version Developed, more IndoRise Studios crew joint to help making the Texture Pack. We also made some Minecraft skins for you to use in-game.
